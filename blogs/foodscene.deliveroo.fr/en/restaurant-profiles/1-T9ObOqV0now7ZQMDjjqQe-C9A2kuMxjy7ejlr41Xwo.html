Inspired by the hot dog carts of New York City, Manhattan Hot Dog gives you a true taste of the States. Bringing together age-old American dishes with the super-hip street food revolution, there has never been a better time to enjoy hot dogs. Shunning the trend to 'go gourmet', this place is all about keeping it real, serving authentic US-style classics with a side of urban cool.  

The famous American 'dogs'

A post shared by Manhattan Hot Dog (@manhattanhotdog) on Feb 7, 2017 at 9:44am PST

Of course, its menu is heavy on the humble hot dog – although there's nothing humble about these sausages. The masterpieces include the famous American Chilidog, with its spicy sauce and rich cheddar cheese. Then there's the Bacondog – hot dog, grilled streaky bacon, tomato sauce, and that bright yellow almost-fluorescent American mustard.

You've also got the devilishly decadent Dog Raclette – a true love story of ingredients. Hot dog meets diced roast potatoes with a kick of fresh chives, before being completely smothered with a gorgeously gooey cheese raclette. And they all lived happily ever after.

However, there's also a mouth-watering build-your-own menu, so you can create your own signature hot dog. Crispy onions.Sweet and smoky barbecue sauce. Creamy coleslaw. Even macaroni and cheese. The only right way to do a hot dog is your way.

New York, New York

A post shared by Manhattan Hot Dog (@manhattanhotdog) on Jan 24, 2017 at 9:09am PST

Manhattan Hot Dog is the brainchild of two childhood friends, Laurent Spinazzola and Serge Padovano. On a trip to New York way back in 2002, American street food seduced them. So they explored the carts and street vendors of 5th Avenue to Wall Street to Central Park, and wanted to bring the American experience back home with them.

After a couple of years of eating their way through New York – a tough job, but someone's got to do it – they perfected their top-secret recipe, and Manhattan Hot Dog opened its doors in France.

Savoury bagels and sugary treats

A post shared by Manhattan Hot Dog (@manhattanhotdog) on Feb 3, 2017 at 9:08am PST

But don't be fooled by the name – Manhattan Hot Dog's menu offers much more than the mighty hot dog. For a lighter lunch, you could try a traditional American bagel.

There's sophisticated smoked salmon, served with cream cheese, chives and a squeeze of sharp lemon juice to bring it all together. We love the Egg Double Bacon too – smoked streaky bacon topped with a fried egg and sprinkled with parmesan and cheddar cheese. If you're looking for a meatier brunch, opt for the Bagel Kebab – veal, turkey and chicken, with crisp salad and sautéed onions and a delicate white sauce.

They also serve a full-on sugar-hit of American candy. Cookies, donuts, muffins and soft doughy pretzels are topped with M&Ms, Snickers, and those iconic Reese's Pieces, taking dessert to a whole new level of indulgence.
