Forget everything you think you know about healthy eating – it's not just all salads and kale smoothies now. cojean is here to show you that being good doesn't mean being boring when it comes to food. From breakfast through to dinner, you'll always find a nutritious meal waiting for you here.

From breakfast in bed to office lunch

A post shared by cojean (@cojeanrestaurants) on Jul 18, 2016 at 10:54pm PDT

For breakfast, grab a pot of their Coco Paradise Granola – layers of coconut milk, fresh fruit, agave syrup, cranberries and much more top this vegan-friendly, gluten-free granola. And if that doesn't do it for you, there's always some Almond Milk Porridge to power you up for the day.

If you need a bit more of a kick for the afternoon instead, there's a whole host of great lunch options here. Try their refreshing Mediterranean Pasta Salad filled with homemade pasta and salty feta cheese and olives, or opt for their Toasted Club Chicken Thai sandwich for all those subtle Asian flavours served up on organic bread.

If you're struggling to decide what to eat in the office, or your boss has asked you to put together a last-minute business lunch, cojean and their mini sandwich menu will be your saviour. You can order classic combinations like tuna, cucumber and chive, or chicken with parmesan and arugula, while for vegetarians we love their fragrant tofu and basil sandwich.

A healthier take on fast food

A post shared by cojean (@cojeanrestaurants) on May 5, 2015 at 11:28pm PDT

cojean first opened its doors in 2001 in Paris, built on a passion of healthy eating and clean living. And since then, they've shared that passion every day to the good people of Paris. Their simple and wholesome foods have caught one quickly, and we think the fact that there's now more than 20 locations across our capital, and a few dotted around other European cities like London, speaks for itself.

Guilt-free as well as fun

A post shared by cojean (@cojeanrestaurants) on Mar 31, 2017 at 8:25am PDT

Just because these guys are all about healthy eating, it doesn't mean they don't love desserts like the rest of us. You'll find goodies that are as rich and sumptuous as usual, made to be health-conscious. They bake cakes every morning here, but the main question is will you choose carrot, lemon and poppy seed, or pistachio and chocolate?

Another of our dessert favourites is their light and fluffy cheesecake, served with a sharp raspberry coulis on a crumbly shortbread biscuit base. Dial down the guilt and order one of their cold pressed juices too  – these organic, nourishing drinks will give you an energy boost without any added sugar.
